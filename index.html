Tenacious Tanks - Sound Showcase

Our BIGGEST project yet! This game was developed by me and three friends as our diploma project. It's intended to be played by up to four players but in this showcase you only see one so you can listen to the sounds better. I am the sound designer of the game and every sound you hear (except the music) was either recorded by me or from a sound library and mixed in Adobe Audition. After that they were implemented in the game with the Wwise Audio Engine. You can find more information on the games officiall instagram here.
